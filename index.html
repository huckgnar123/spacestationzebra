What do we do?

We are Space Station Zebra! That's what we do! Ok, We are a retro game company that offers exciting stories and old school game play. We are also looking to include collectibles in our games. Whether that may be artwork in the form of characters, items, locations and more. The modern touch to our games will be that all collectibles will be owned by the user and fully tradeable on the Ethereum blockchain.
